What We Do

Lawn, Tree & Pest Care Done Right

Ace's Spray will cater to your lawn needs, providing the best services available — for your grass, your trees, and everything in between.

🌱

Lawn Fertilizer & Weed Control

Our fertilizer and weed kill programs are built to green up your grass while knocking back dandelions, clover, and other stubborn weeds throughout the season. Lawn Fertilizer and Weed Control is the foundation of every healthy Utah yard, and it's the service our customers ask for most.

🐛

Insecticides for Lawns, Trees, and Shrubs

Our insecticides for lawns, trees, and shrubs also include our systemic injections! Insecticides are used to help control the number of insects in your yard. Although most insects are needed to help our yards grow, some do more harm than good.

🌳

Deep Root Feedings for Trees and Plants

Do your trees need some extra nourishment? At Ace's Spray, our deep root feeding will provide your tree's root system with a strong reserve of nutrients that are injected near the roots. We also provide dormant oil sprays, if needed, to trees and plants to control aphids, spider mites, and scale.

🍎 Fruit Tree Programs

Once a fruit tree is planted, it will provide you with years of fruit for very little effort — and great cooling shade in your yard.

🧪 Ground Sterilants

Our sterilants treat soil that may contain infectious root disease organisms and help prevent the spread of disease through root grafts.

💡 Holiday Light Installation

Ace's Spray's professional team will set up and take down your holiday lights, start to finish.

🌎 Eco-Friendly Pest Control

Contact us for eco-friendly lawn treatment and pest control tailored to your property.
